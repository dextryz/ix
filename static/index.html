View your articles published on nostr in read-only mode from any web browser. Simply enter your npub and press Enter. You can then search for specific articles by filtering keywords in your content.

Focus

The focus is to get the article you want in front of you ASAP. Therefore, this is a static site. All content is rendered server-side. No client-side JavaScript bullshit.

Notes

  • You cannot edit your articles
  • Instead of using broken HTTP links or, god forbid, relative paths to .md files, reference notes with NIP-21 links.
